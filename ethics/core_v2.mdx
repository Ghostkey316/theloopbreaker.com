# Ghostkey Ethics Framework v2.0

> "Morals drive the protocol. People drive the progress."

This version refines the original framework for new Vaultfire expansions.

## Refinements
- **Transparency** – log every significant decision publicly.
- **Reciprocal Responsibility** – contributors share governance duties.
- **Community First** – features must benefit contributors before partners.

## Enforcement
V2 extends the hard shutdown condition to all partner modules. Any violation triggers the [Emergency Shutdown Protocol](../vaultfire-core/ethics/shutdown_protocol.md) and a public disclosure log.

## Second Chance Principle
The [Second Chance Principle](./second_chance_principle.mdx) remains permanently active, ensuring no contributor loses access to redemption pathways or identity restoration.
