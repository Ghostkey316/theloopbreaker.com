## Risk Mitigation

### Risk: Nobody responds to outreach
**Mitigation:**
- Increase volume (200+ contacts instead of 100)
- Improve subject lines (A/B test)
- Leverage warm intros (ask existing network)
- Try different channels (LinkedIn, Twitter, Telegram)

### Risk: Demos don't convert
**Mitigation:**
- Practice demo script 10x
- Record yourself, find weaknesses
- Address objections faster
- Shorten demo to 10 minutes (people's attention is short)

### Risk: Partners want custom work
**Mitigation:**
- "Happy to customize for strategic partners (equity/large contract)"
- "For integrations, we have a standard 5-minute setup"
- Don't say yes to everything - focus on scalable partnerships

### Risk: Slow decision-making
**Mitigation:**
- Create urgency (grant deadlines, competitor pressure)
- Offer pilot (low-commitment way to try)
- Executive sponsor (find internal champion)
