**Be radically transparent:**

- "The code exists and it's production-ready. I need someone who can deploy it and scale it."
- "I have validation from Base ecosystem (poet.base.eth, Xen, Roon). That's not easy to get."

**What to look for:**
- Excited by the MISSION, not just the tech
- Experience deploying to Base/Ethereum L2s
- Can talk to investors/auditors confidently
- Willing to take 50/50 equity (not 80/20)

**Red flags:**
- Wants to "rebuild from scratch" (you have working code!)
- Dismissive of ecosystem engagement ("that doesn't matter")
- Wants majority equity despite you having validation

### Questions to Ask Them

1. "Have you deployed smart contracts to Base before?"
2. "Can you explain the circuit breaker pattern in our codebase?"
3. "How would you approach the first 90 days?"
4. "What's your experience with fundraising/grants?"
5. "Are you all-in or is this a side project?"

- If someone asks "How did you implement the circuit breaker?" → "I worked with a technical advisor. I can connect you with them."
- Be transparent: "I'm the vision/storytelling, seeking technical execution partner."

❌ **Don't get paralyzed by imposter syndrome**
- You have something REAL (poet.base.eth doesn't like 20+ posts from randos)
- The narrative resonates because it's authentic
- Pizza guy → protocol founder is a BETTER story than Stanford → protocol founder

❌ **Don't give away equity cheaply**
- 50/50 with a true co-founder = fair
- 10% for "advisor" who does nothing = bad deal
- If in doubt, ask in Base Discord or DM poet.base.eth

❌ **Don't wait for permission**
- You don't need Base's "approval" to ship (it's open-source)
- You don't need investor validation to find creators
- Action > perfection
