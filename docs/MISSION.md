## The Architectural Guarantee

**1. No Central Authority**
- Protocol is open-source and decentralized
- No company can unilaterally change the rules
- No government can weaponize it for control
- No platform can extract rents or manipulate outcomes

**3. Voluntary Participation**
- Creators opt-in to receive rewards

- No one is forced to participate

- PoP scoring rewards authenticity, not compliance
- No "social punishment" mechanisms
- Positive-sum game (creators + supporters both win)

- All code is open-source (GitHub)
- All rules are published and immutable
- No hidden algorithms or black-box scoring
- Community can verify fairness
