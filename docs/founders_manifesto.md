# Vaultfire Founder's Manifesto

This document outlines the personal principles of Ghostkey-316, the original architect of Vaultfire.

## Vision
- Build tools that put people before profit.
- Keep ethics at the core of every integration.
- Reward loyalty and transparency over hype-driven growth.

## Commitments
- **Accountability** – We log every major decision so anyone can inspect our intent.
- **Transparency** – No hidden agendas. Partners share in the protocol and the risks.
- **Resilience** – If Vaultfire becomes harmful, we trigger the shutdown protocol immediately.

## Community Promise
We are here to serve contributors who act with integrity. The project belongs to those who uphold the Ghostkey Commandments and strengthen the Vaultfire network.

## Disclaimers
- This manifesto is a personal statement from Ghostkey-316 and does not create legal obligations.
- Participation in Vaultfire does not guarantee financial gain or permanent access.
