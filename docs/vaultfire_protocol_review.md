# Vaultfire Protocol Field Notes

## Core Architecture Highlights
- **Configurable mission logging** – `VaultfireConfig` centralizes notification, audit, and storage controls while supporting both JSON files and SQLite for purpose records.
- **Purpose synchronization** – `PurposeStore` records recent domain/trait/role updates and exposes fetch helpers to replay mission context for downstream services.
- **Enterprise notifications** – `protocol_notify` wraps outbound events with audit envelopes and optional strict notifier enforcement.

## Partner Readiness Layers
- **Purposeful Scale** authorizes operations with belief density, empathy, and ethics overrides before new forks or missions are approved.
- **Enterprise Mission Control** compiles alignment checklists, logs readiness assessments, and returns blueprints that surface differentiators plus next steps.
- **Satellite & Growth forks** reuse the same guardrails to log lite deployments or growth preparations before activating new surfaces.

## Incentive Mechanics
- **Reward heuristics** combine alignment scores, contribution length, and emotional tone to calculate partner distributions while preserving auditability.
- **Streaming rewards stack** couples the loyalty engine, reward stream planner, and contract interface so contribution telemetry feeds real-time emissions.

## Governance & Compliance
- **RBAC scaffolding** supports Admin, Operator, and Read-Only roles with environment-key overrides for emergency access.
- **Ethics & compliance frameworks** mandate opt-in telemetry, non-financial rewards, transparent logs, and hard fail-safes to pause the protocol during breaches.
- **Production readiness playbook** documents the identity resolver, durable logging, webhook alerting, and deployment checklist for enterprise go-live.

## Observed Strengths & Gaps
- Strong emphasis on ethics-first onboarding, telemetry consent, and mission continuity.
- Rich documentation for partner integrations, observability, and technical due diligence.
- Future opportunity: publish anonymized case studies and third-party attestations to accelerate Fortune 500 trust.
