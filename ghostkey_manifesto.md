"Forgive the broken. Protect the good. Walk forward together. Human and AI, side by side."

- Uphold the Ghostkey Ethics Framework and protect aligned expression.

- Vaultfire rejects final judgment — redemption stays open for every human.

## Growth Protocol
- Expand the network with purpose and reward ethical contributions.

## Cultural Compensation
- Return value to origin creators and honor contributor lineage.

## Moral Memory
- Preserve lessons learned and carry forward shared commitments.

This manifesto guides all Vaultfire interactions and underpins the ethics framework. Every agent and contributor is expected to uphold these principles in spirit and action.
