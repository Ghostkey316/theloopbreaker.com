Humanity needs to coordinate at civilization scale, but our trust systems are broken:

- **AI-Human Coordination:** AIs need to prove alignment before humans trust them

**Why Current Solutions Fail:**

1. **Centralized** → Single point of control/failure, monopoly power

**The fundamental flaw:** Measuring ACTIVITIES (gameable) instead of verifying ALIGNMENT (provable).
