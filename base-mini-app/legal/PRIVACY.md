# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** January 10, 2026

## Our Commitment

Vaultfire is a **privacy-first protocol**. We collect zero personal data. Period.

## What We Collect

**Nothing.** 

Specifically:
- ❌ No cookies
- ❌ No trackers
- ❌ No analytics
- ❌ No email addresses
- ❌ No personal information
- ❌ No IP address logging
- ❌ No behavioral tracking

## What Is Public

Due to the nature of blockchain technology, the following information is publicly visible on the Base blockchain:

- ✅ **Your wallet address** (public by blockchain design)
- ✅ **Belief hashes** (SHA256 hashes of your belief text)
- ✅ **Transaction hashes** (Base blockchain transaction IDs)
- ✅ **Timestamps** (when attestations were created)
- ✅ **Module IDs** (which verification module was used)

**Important:** Your actual belief text is **never** stored anywhere. Only the cryptographic hash is recorded on-chain.

## What Stays Private

Thanks to zero-knowledge proofs, the following information remains completely private:

- 🔒 **Belief text** (never leaves your browser)
- 🔒 **Loyalty scores** (hidden in ZK proof)
- 🔒 **Private keys** (never transmitted to our servers)
- 🔒 **Personal identity** (wallet-only, no KYC)

## Third-Party Services

### WalletConnect

We use WalletConnect for wallet connections. WalletConnect may collect:
- Wallet connection metadata (for session management)
- IP addresses (for relay server functionality)

See [WalletConnect Privacy Policy](https://walletconnect.com/privacy) for details.

### Base Network

All transactions are recorded on the Base blockchain. Base (operated by Coinbase) may collect:
- Transaction data (public blockchain data)
- RPC request metadata (IP addresses, timing)

See [Base Privacy Policy](https://base.org/privacy) for details.

## Data Storage

We do not operate any servers that store your data. All data is either:
1. **Client-side only** (your browser, your device)
2. **On-chain** (Base blockchain, public and immutable)

## Your Rights

Since we collect no personal data, there is nothing to:
- Request access to
- Request deletion of
- Request correction of
- Export or port

Your wallet is your identity. You control it 100%.

## Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children. Since we collect zero personal data, there are no special provisions needed for children's privacy.

## Changes to This Policy

If we ever change our privacy practices (e.g., add optional analytics), we will:
1. Update this document
2. Update the "Last Updated" date
3. Announce changes in our repository

## Compliance

This privacy policy is designed to comply with:
- ✅ **GDPR** (EU General Data Protection Regulation)
- ✅ **CCPA** (California Consumer Privacy Act)
- ✅ **PIPEDA** (Canadian privacy law)

Since we collect no personal data, compliance is inherent to our design.
